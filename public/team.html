Your Team

Jakob Sanderson

President

Jakob is a fifth year Political Studies and Economics student passionate about student leadership. A former Arts Student Body President and Current UMSU President, Jakob began the first participatory budgeting project on campus which increased arts students’ bursary fund by 50%. On UMSU, he hosted the organization’s inaugural Financial Aid Awareness Week and successfully lobbied to keep interest off our student loans. This prospective environmental lawyer, passionate about a campus composting plan, cares about your money and the planet - but neither is the green closest to Jakob’s heart. That spot will always be claimed by his beloved Boston Celtics.

Sarah Bonner-Proulx

Vice-President
Advocacy

Coming from a small town in northern Manitoba, Sarah knows how big of a transition University can be and challenges students face. From striving to improve your university experience during her time as a Resident Advisor, to using her platform as Active Minds co-president and to advocate for improved mental health supports and ensure that all students feel safe and welcomed here on campus- she has always been consistent in her desire for improving the lives of her fellow students. By re-electing her as VP Advocacy, she will take her extensive experience working with students and use it to elevate your voice and hear your concerns to ensure that your university experience is a positive one. When Sarah isnt in the boardroom fighting for students, you can find her in her natural habitat: kicking back somewhere in nature with her dog, Sasha or sipping on a cup of dark roast, rattling off bad dad-jokes.

Kyra Fanning

Vice-President
Community Engagement

Kyra has spent the last four years working towards a double major in marketing and supply chain. As this years Business Banquet Co-Chair, on the Commerce Students’ Association, through innovative thinking and leadership she had the fastest sell out in history and doubled profits from the previous year. Kyra has also successfully increased membership by 430% and corporate sponsorship by 200% through her vice presidency of the University of Manitoba Supply Chain Organization. Passionate about the community, Kyra has volunteered for the Shinerama and 5 Days campaigns to offer support and raise awareness. In this new role, Kyra will be an asset to help every student take full advantage of the programming UMSU has to offer. In her spare time, Kyra loves to golf and whether its on or off the course, she always brings her A-game.

Mbuli Matshe

Vice-President
Finance & Operations

When Mbuli is not playing Fifa, or watching his favorite soccer team (Arsenal, of course) you can find him immersed in all things finance. As the Arts Finance Director (ASBC), the current VP of Finance and Operations for UMSU, and by serving on multiple other budgetary committees, he has gained invaluable experience in balancing budgets, and managing finances to enable you - the student body - to have access to the best services and programming possible. He wants to continue to serve behind the scenes, like Batman, to enable you to have the best year yet.

Jelynn Dela Cruz

Vice-President
Student Life

While Jelynn isn’t belting Celine Dion on karaoke, her heart still goes on exploring the many sides that our campus has to offer. In her experience, and despite her cyborg left knee, she has helped students from different walks of life through her presidencies for both the revamped Sociology and Criminology Association and the U of M’s founding Peace of Mind chapter. By her extensive volunteering she has had the opportunity to work with the city-wide community - and hopes to bring her creativity and compassionate heart to the role of VP Student Life. As she’s done for underprivileged classrooms in both the Philippines and Winnipeg’s inner city, Jelynn wants to ensure that you have the proper resources and supports to help you find your calling within the U of M community - in order to have the best and most enjoyable experience possible!
